Cornell Thrift

Reducing waste through thrifting, one item at a time.

Our Mission

Our goal is to reduce campus-wide waste through redistribution of reusable personal items, inspired by the concept of “free-piles” seen in university co‐operative housing, along with the annual Dump-and-Run sale. We want to see reusable clothes, homeware, electronics, and small furniture going into homes and not trashcans – all year round.
